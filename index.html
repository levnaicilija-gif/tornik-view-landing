Our Story

A mountain you can call yours

Some places hold you the moment you arrive. The hush of pines. The crisp note of mountain air. The bright line of Tornik against a winter sky.

Tornik View was born from that pull. A small enclave at Kobilja Glava — eight private triplex residences across four discreet buildings, set between the ski slopes of Tornik and the running waters of the Crni Rzav.

Not a hotel. Not a complex. Just eight homes for those who want the mountain to feel like theirs.

— Tornik View

The Project

Eight residences. Four buildings. One mountain.

Phase One of Tornik View is intentionally small. Four discreet buildings, each holding two triplex residences. Eight owners in total — not eighty, not eight hundred. A measured project, designed to keep the privacy of the forest intact.
